How To Play

The deck. 24 cards: 9 through Ace in each suit. You and the seat across from you (North) are partners against East and West.

Bidding. Each hand, a card is turned up. In round 1, players may order it up (dealer picks it up as trump). If everyone passes, round 2 lets anyone name a different suit as trump.

Bowers. The Jack of trump (right bower) is the top card. The same-color Jack (left bower) is second, and counts as trump.

Tricks. Highest card of the suit led wins, unless trumped. Winner leads next. Five tricks per hand.

Scoring. Making team gets 1 point (3–4 tricks), 2 points (all 5, a "march"), or 4 points for a lone march. If the makers fail, the defenders score 2 ("euchred").

First to 10 points wins the game.
